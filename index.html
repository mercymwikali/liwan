About Liwan

At Liwan Fresh, we specialize in the cultivation and global distribution of a wide variety of fruits, such as avocados, pineapples, mangoes, passion fruits, and coconuts sourced from Kenya. With a presence in more than 20 countries worldwide, we blend our extensive years of experience with innovative concepts to provide a novel perspective on the export of agricultural produce. We firmly believe that grasping customer needs is at the core of effectively executing export initiatives.
